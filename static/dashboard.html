Record enrichment · where the time goes

Each backend model's per-record time, split into the model's own compute — a floor set by the model and hardware — and time spent waiting for a free slot when the system is busy. A bar that is mostly floor is limited by the model; a large wait segment means it is limited by load, and the fix is more capacity or less concurrency, not a faster model.
